What's This?

Welcome to Drap's Labs! This is a small personal web/server tech project made by Drapadon and Digytt. We are some tech enthusiasts who built this project for it's usefulness, but also for our friend community, and most of all, really just for fun. Drap's Labs originally started as Drapadon's home server, however the hardware it ran on wasn't great. After some time, he upgraded hardware and purchased a domain, and now we have the power to run fun projects like this. We hope you take the time to look around and enjoy our creation. Thanks for stopping by.
